Bunjee Jumping in Nepal

Nepal is a paradise for adventure lovers, and one of the most thrilling experiences here is bungee jumping. If you are an adrenaline junkie, then this is an experience you donâ€™t want to miss!

The most famous bungee jumping spot in Nepal is at The Last Resort, located near the Bhotekoshi River. This site features a jump from a 160-meter high suspension bridge, making it one of the highest bungee jumps in the world. The breathtaking surroundings of lush green hills and the roaring river below make the experience even more unforgettable.

Another exciting location is the Kushma Bungee, which is the second-highest bungee jump in the world, with a height of 228 meters. Located in the Baglung-Pokhara region, this spot offers a thrilling free fall with stunning views of the deep gorge and the Himalayas.

Safety is a top priority, and both sites follow international safety standards, ensuring a secure and exhilarating jump. Professional instructors guide you through the process, making it safe even for first-timers.

If you love adventure sports, bungee jumping in Nepal is a must-try activity that guarantees an unforgettable rush of excitement!
